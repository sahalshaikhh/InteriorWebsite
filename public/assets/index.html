Frequently Asked Questions

What are the key elements of modern interior design? 🔻
Modern interior design often includes clean lines, open spaces, and a neutral color palette. It focuses on simplicity, functionality, and the use of modern materials.
How can Roman design be incorporated into modern interiors? 🔻
Roman design can be incorporated through the use of classical architectural elements, such as columns and arches, as well as luxurious materials like marble and mosaic. A balance between modern and Roman elements can create a unique and elegant interior.
